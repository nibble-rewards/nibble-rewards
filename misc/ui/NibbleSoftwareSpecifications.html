Nibble Specifications - Merchant

Essentially, the Nibble platform allows a Merchant to:

  1. Create a Reward Campaign
  2. Review the campaign stats
  3. Receive email alerts on campaign stats

Dashboard menu will consist of:

  1. Campaign - Starting a loyalty campaign and reviewing status/progress.
  2. POS Connection - The Square account that the merchant is connected to (I am unsure of this - I will need verification from Adam/Rob to determine this)
  3. Account - Updating account information and notification alerts
  4. Logout - Logout of account

CAMPAIGN

We are going to go over two different sections:

  1. Starting a loyalty campaign
  2. When a loyalty campaign is currently active

1. Starting a loyalty campaign

Fields:

  1. Selecting the number of stars: 5 or 10
  2. Minimum Purchase: Dollar Amount
  3. Reward: Dollar Amount

2. When a loyalty campaign is currently active

Same layout as Merchant Dashboard > Customers > Loyalty

Three different Sections:

  1. Loyalty Overview
  2. Loyalty Card Status
  3. Recent Transactions
  4. Edit/Delete Action

A. Loyalty Overview

Status: Active since (Date Campaign Started)

Number of Loyalty Stars: 5 or 10

Reward: (Reward) off next purchase

Minimum Purchase: (Minimum Purchase)

B. Loyalty Card Status

Same as Merchant Dashboard

C. Recent Transactions

Transaction links:

There is a link for every transaction. When clicked, it takes the merchant to Merchant Dashboard > Sales > Transactions Specific Transaction

4. Edit/Delete Action

Same as editing or deleting reward in Square Merchant Dashboard
